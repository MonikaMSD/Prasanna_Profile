About Me

Ph.D. Scholar at the University of Galway started in Oct 2023. From Feb 2023 to Sep 2023, worked as a Research Assistant at Insight SFI Research Centre for Data Analytics, Data Science Institute, University of Galway, Ireland. I completed my master's degree (MSc in Machine Intelligence) at the Indian Institute of Information Technology and Management-Kerala, India, from 2020 to 2022. I also worked as a research intern on Dravidian Languages in the domain of NLP. My research areas are machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, under-resourced languages, sentiment analysis, offensive language, and hate speech. I have published multiple international conferences (LDK, FIRE, etc.) and highly reputed journal papers (LRE, CSL). I am organizing numerous shared tasks related to social media text analysis in the Association of Computational Linguistics (ACL 2022) and the European Association of Computational Linguistics (EACL 2021). I have an h-index of 08, an i10-index of 08, and citations of 316 from Google Scholar as of 2022.
